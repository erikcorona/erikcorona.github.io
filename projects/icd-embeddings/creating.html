Let's Create ICD Embeddings!

We use a BERT-style masked language model to learn ICD embeddings. The model takes a patient's claim history as a sequence of up to 512 positions, masks 15% of ICD codes, and learns to predict them from surrounding clinical context. This forces the model to learn meaningful relationships between diagnoses, procedures, costs, and patient characteristics.
