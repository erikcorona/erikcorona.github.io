Let's Create ICD Embeddings!

We use a BERT-style masked language model to learn ICD embeddings. The model takes a patient's claim history as a sequence of up to 512 positions, masks 15% of ICD codes, and learns to predict them from surrounding clinical context.

Model Overview
A 29.9M parameter transformer that reads a patient's claim history and learns to predict masked ICD diagnosis codes from surrounding context. Each position in the sequence combines 10 input features into a 299-dimensional vector.
